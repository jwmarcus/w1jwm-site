Amateur Radio Guides from W1JWM

Station Operation Modes

I operate on phone and digital modes on the HF frequencies and higher, such as:

  • Digital VHF/UHF - DMR via NEDECN/DMR-MARC (TG:3181)
  • Packet VHF/UHF - AX.25, LinBPQ32
  • Digital HF - RTTY, FT-8, JS8Call, WSPR
  • FM Repeaters - Usually on the W1BOS Repeater
  • FM Simplex - 146.520 around QTH
  • EchoLink - NEWENG2 or IRLP Node 9127
  • FM Simplex - 2.4 GHz 802.11 P2P experimental modes

Station Activities

I enjoy getting outdoors, experimenting with different load-outs, handling NTS traffic, and working public service events.

  • ARRL traffic handling (also fill-in Net Control)
  • Volunteering (Boston Marathon, BAA 10k, Head of the Charles, Run of the Charles)
  • Antenna design & construction
  • Off-grid, man-portable digital system design

Station Capabilities

I am usually operating portable, but I can generally be found on the following bands:

  • 13cm
  • 70cm
  • 2m
  • 20m
  • 40m
  • 80m (rarely)
